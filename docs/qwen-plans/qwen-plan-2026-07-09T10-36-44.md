### 1. **Severe Register Pressure → Spilling** | Severity: High | Effort: M

**File:** `gated_delta_net_chunk.cu:79-84`

**Issue:**
```cpp

gfx906 (Vega 20) has **256 VGPRs per SIMD**. With 450+ registers needed, the compiler will spill ~200 registers to scratch memory (L1 cache), causing severe performance degradation. This explains why the kernel works but may underperform expectations.

```
